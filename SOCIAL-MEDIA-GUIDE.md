# Social Media Automation Guide - X4O (Pty) Limited

## Overview
Automated weekly Facebook posts to maintain consistent online presence.

## Recommended Tool: Meta Business Suite (Free)

### Setup Steps
1. Go to https://business.facebook.com
2. Log in with the Facebook account that manages the X4O page
3. Navigate to **Planner** (left sidebar)
4. Click **Create Post** > **Facebook Post**
5. Write your post content, add an image
6. Click **Schedule** instead of Publish
7. Set the date/time for the post
8. Click **Schedule Post**

### Weekly Content Calendar Template

| Week | Topic | Content Idea |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Consulting | Highlight a consulting service (e.g., Project Management tips) |
| 2 | Coaching | Share coaching success story or testimonial |
| 3 | Industry | Share relevant industry news or insights (renewable energy, digital transformation) |
| 4 | Company | Company updates, team highlights, event announcements |

## Alternative: Buffer (Free Tier)
- URL: https://buffer.com
- Free tier: 3 social channels, 10 scheduled posts per channel
- Supports Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn
- Has browser extension for quick sharing
- Analytics dashboard included

### Buffer Setup
1. Sign up at buffer.com
2. Connect your Facebook Page
3. Set posting schedule (e.g., every Wednesday at 10:00 AM SAST)
4. Add posts to queue throughout the week
5. Buffer publishes automatically on schedule

## Content Best Practices
- Use high-quality images (1200x630px for Facebook)
- Keep text concise (under 250 characters for best engagement)
- Include a clear call-to-action
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Post between 9 AM - 12 PM SAST for best reach
- Engage with comments within 24 hours
- Mix content types: 40% educational, 30% promotional, 30% engagement
